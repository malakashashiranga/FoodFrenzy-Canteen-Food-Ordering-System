Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Here are some common questions and answers:

  • How do I place an order?
    To place an order, firstly, you need to register on the platform. Then, simply browse our menu, select the items you'd like, and proceed to checkout.
  • Can I modify or cancel my order?
    No, you can't modify or cancel your order after placing it. However, you can make changes or cancel items in your cart before finalizing the order.
  • Is delivery available?
    No, we do not offer delivery services at the moment.
